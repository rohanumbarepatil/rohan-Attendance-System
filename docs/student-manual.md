# Student Manual

## Dashboard
Live attended/missed counts, overall percentage, subject-wise chart, and a red banner if you fall below the defaulter threshold. Figures update the moment faculty submit attendance.

## My Attendance
Three tabs:
- **Date-wise** — every lecture with its status
- **Subject-wise** — attended/conducted and percentage per subject
- **Monthly** — month-by-month percentages

Categories: Excellent (>90%), Good (>75%), Warning (<75%), Critical (<60%).

## Reports
Generate your own daily/weekly/monthly/semester/yearly report and download it as PDF or Excel.

## Notifications
Bell icon shows unread count; the Notifications page lists attendance confirmations, low-attendance warnings, defaulter alerts and announcements — all delivered in real time while you are signed in.

## Profile
Update your display name and phone. Email and role are managed by the administrator.
