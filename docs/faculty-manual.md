# Faculty Manual

## Dashboard
Your assigned subjects, total sessions, today's sessions and recent activity. Use the **Mark** shortcut beside any subject.

## Marking Attendance
1. Mark Attendance → choose subject, date, lecture number.
2. The class roster (students of the subject's semester/department) loads with everyone preset to Present.
3. Toggle P / A / L / ML / AL per student, or use *All Present* / *All Absent*.
4. **Submit & Lock** — the session is saved atomically and locked. Re-submitting the same subject+date+lecture is rejected.
5. Each student receives an "Attendance marked" notification automatically.

## Editing & History
History lists your sessions. Open one to view records and edit a status with a **reason** — every change is stored in the record's modification history and the audit log. Locked sessions need an admin unlock first.

## Defaulters
Live list of students under the threshold. **Send Warning** delivers an in-app alert instantly.

## Students & Reports
Search any student and view their per-subject summary. Reports support all periods with PDF/Excel download.
